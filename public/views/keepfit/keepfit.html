KeepFit

Track your walking goals on Android

  • Add goals in miles, yards, meters, or kilometers.
  • View a history of your walking progress.
  • Check statistics on how you've advanced.
  • Use a built in step counting algorithm (expiremental!) to record your steps.

This app is not available on the Play Store, but you can view and download the source from GitHub try it out.
