Bramble

A music playing app with artist image chooser

  • Find music from artists, albums, and songs.
  • Automatically finds images for artists in your library.
  • If an artist's image is wrong, search for a new replacement.
  • Tap or slide up on the bottom song bar to focus on whatever's Now Playing.
  • Parallax scrolling!

This app is not available on the Play Store, but you can view and download the source from GitHub to try it out.
